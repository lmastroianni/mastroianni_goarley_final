Who We Are

For the enhancement, protection and promotion
of a viable, multi-species fishery within the Thames River watershed.

Rivers Helped

The TRAA works extensively with the Thames River watershed as well as smaller local creeks and streams to ensure they are fit for fish.

840,000 +

Fish Stocked

Since 2014 we have stocked local rivers and streams with over 840,000 fish to help keep a steadly growing population of trout.

Events

The club has many different events like rod building classes, a trout hatchery and many more fun events for you to become involved.
